If you are a heavy Google Reader user then you are probably looking for a desktop feed reader that lets you easily access and manage your RSS subscriptions. Liferea is a simple feed reader that brings your RSS subscriptions right into your desktop.

Liferea comes with a lot of useful features such as:

  • Offline support 
  • Google Reader & TinyTinyRSS synchronization
  • Google Reader labels/tags support
  • Ability to import/export OPML files
  • One-click favicons update

Liferea integrates well with Unity:

Liferea's counter badge displaying the number of unread news

Liferea is available in the Official Ubuntu Repositories. To install it, run this command:

sudo apt-get install liferea
