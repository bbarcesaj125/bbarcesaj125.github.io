ffDiaporama is an application that lets you create video sequences using photos, music, videos files and text (i.e. titles). Think of it as a movie creator that allows you to produce slideshows by inserting smooth transition between two or more video tracks.

ffDiaporama is a fork of Videoporama that was totally rewritten from the ground up in Qt. It comes bundled with a lot of features such as:


  • Ability to cut/rearrange videos clips
  • Video cutting
  • Image filters (Gama, lumunosity, contrast ...)
  • Animation support (Zoom, rotation, Ken Burn Effect ...)
  • Image/Video resizing
  • Wide range of supported image/video formats (Bmp, gif, jpeg ... /Mkv, avi, mp4 ...)
  • Sound support
  • Preview mode (Real time)
