Great Little Radio Player (or GLRP) is an application that offers you the possibility to listen to a huge list  (over 300 stations) of online radio stations without using a web browser. It comes with a clean Qt interface and lots of features such as:

  • Large collection of radio stations
  • Notfiy-OSD desktop notifications
  • App Indicator support
  • Ability to add new radio stations
  • Filter system (e.g. sort stations by country, genre ...)
