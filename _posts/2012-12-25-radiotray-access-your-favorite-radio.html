RadioTray is an small application that allows you to listen to your favorite radio stations from the App Indicator area. It aims to provide a very minimalistic interface, thus making it a distraction-free application.

Here is a list of RadioTray features:

  • Plugin collection 
  • Playlist support (PLS, M3U, ASX, WAX and WVX formats)
  • Bookmarks support (Drag & Drop)
  • NotifyOSD notifications
