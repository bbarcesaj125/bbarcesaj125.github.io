Nautilus Flickr Uploader is a tiny application that lets you easily upload your favorite pictures to Flickr. It is a very simple tool that does exactly what you need it to do: It allows you to send your photos to Flickr with just a few steps.

Nautilus Flickr Uploader offers the following features:
  • Simple and clean interface
  • Tagging support
  • Privacy settings (control the target: public, private, friends/family ...)
  • Drag & Drop upload
When you start Nautilus Flickr Uploader for the first time, it will ask your authorization so that it can upload your pictures to Flickr:


The process of authentication is straightforward, just click on "Next" and a browser window will open asking you to link your account to Nautilus Flickr Uploader:


Accept the agreement and Flickr will give you a verification code that you should enter into the verification dialog:


Now that your account is properly linked, you can start uploading your pictures.

To install Nautilus Flickr Uploader on Ubuntu, download the .deb from its official website (HERE), and then run the following command in a terminal:


sudo dpkg -i <FILENAME.deb>
