FileBot is a Java application that lets you easily rename and organize your movie collection. Apart from renaming movies, anime episodes and music, FileBot also comes with additional features such as fetching subtitles from online sources and downloading artwork.

Features:

  • Clean interface with Drag 'N Drop support
  • Command-Line interface
  • Built-in subtitles downloader (from Opensubtitles)
  • Online sources (TheTVDB, AniDB, TvRage, Serienjunkies, TheMovieDB, IMDB ...)
  • Internal subtitle viewer for Srt, Sub and Ass formats
  • Customizable episode renaming algorithm
  • Series name auto-detection
  • AcoustID support (using metadata from MusicBrainz)
